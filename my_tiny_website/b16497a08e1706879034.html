Welcome to Laura Garcia's Portfolio

Laura Garcia is a passionate and experienced Product Designer from Spain. With 5 years of professional experience, Laura has honed her skills in innovative and user-centered design. Her journey began at the prestigious Madrid Design School, where she obtained a Bachelor's degree in Product Design. She then pursued a Masters in Advanced Design, specializing in sustainable product solutions.

Laura's expertise lies in 3D modeling, user research, and implementing eco-friendly design practices. She is fluent in both Spanish and English, which allows her to effectively communicate with clients and colleagues from diverse backgrounds.

During her professional career, Laura has made significant contributions to sustainable design projects as a Junior Product Designer at Verde Design Studio from 2018 to 2020. Her responsibilities included leading design projects, conducting 3D modeling, and providing valuable client consultations.

Currently, Laura holds the role of Senior Product Designer at Creativa Concepts since 2021. In this role, she is responsible for project management, conducting user research, and driving design innovation.

Laura's skills include expertise in 3D modeling and user-centered design, along with proficiency in eco-friendly design practices.
