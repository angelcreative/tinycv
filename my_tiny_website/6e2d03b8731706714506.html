Miguel Cabrera is a product designer with experience in various design tools such as Adobe XD, Figma, FigJam, ProtoPie, Notion, Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, and Cinema 4D. He is skilled in UX methodologies, including user persona creation, wireframing, prototyping, user research, and usability testing.

Miguel has worked as a Junior UX/UI Designer at Catapulta Startup SAS, an incubator for app-based startups in Bogotá, Colombia, from June 2019 to June 2020. During this time, he collaborated closely with development teams and managed a wide range of UI/UX and branding projects for startups.

From June 2020 to December 2020, Miguel worked as a Product Designer at Catapulta Startup SAS. He was responsible for selecting and implementing operational processes and tools and leading the design team. Miguel also worked as a consultant at Tiny Ship SA in Panama City, Panama from February 2021 to October 2021, where he was involved in the design of the Zappiii app for iOS and Android. He focused on enhancing the user experience and also contributed to graphic design, content writing, and social media graphics.

In his role as a UI Designer at Pragma SA, based in Medellín, Colombia, from December 2021 to April 2022, Miguel worked on computational systems and services related to the risk management and credit fraud sector. He also improved the candidate experience during the selection process and conducted talks on design trends and LowCode/NoCode platforms for the community.

Miguel has freelanced as a UX/UI Designer, collaborating closely with development teams on various startup projects. He specializes in adapting visual design elements and evaluating product leads to create appealing designs that enhance the user experience.

Miguel's previous design experience includes working at MATT Manda2, an inclusive and sustainable delivery company, and Parki, an Airbnb-like platform for parking spaces. He studied Graphic Design at the Fundación Universitaria del Área Andina in Colombia.

Miguel has also pursued further education through online courses on platforms such as Udemy and Crehana, focusing on topics like UX design, usability, prototyping, and interactive design.
