Crafting Creative Visions

Jose Maria Mayoral Montilla is a Project Manager at Audiense, a social media and customer services company based in Crdoba, Andalusia, Spain. He holds a degree in Business Administration and Management from Universidad Loyola. With a passion for marketing, social media advertising, and customer services, Jose has been working in these fields since 2012.

Throughout his career, Jose has worked with various clients in vibrant cities like Rome and London. He values each opportunity as a chance to learn and grow, embracing continuous learning to stay ahead in the ever-evolving world of business. Working with clients from diverse industries has not only expanded his knowledge but also allowed him to contribute meaningfully.

Currently, Jose is working as a Project Manager at Audiense, where he is responsible for executing demand projects for key clients such as Banco Pichincha, G2 Esports, Philip Morris International, and Richard Mille. He also provides support to the Customer Success Team and Sales Team.

Prior to his role at Audiense, Jose worked for 8 years as a Support Manager in London, UK, and Crdoba, Spain. In this role, he engaged with customers, handled their emails, analyzed and categorized incidents, and created content for user guides. He also supported the Operations Team in account creation and managing the cancellation process.

In addition to his work at Audiense, Jose is currently a Tourist Apartment Manager at Apartamento Crdoba. In this role, he manages and promotes bookings, coordinates cleaning and maintenance services, provides first-class customer service, ensures compliance with regulations and standards, and encourages positive reviews and feedback.

Before Audiense and Apartamento Crdoba, Jose worked as part of the Marketing and Social Media Team at ROME REPORTS Tv News Agency, where he created content for social media, covered the Conclave 2013 and the first months of Pope Francis' pontificate, and managed campaigns to promote subscriptions to the agency's premium version.

Earlier in his career, Jose worked at Dicesis Crdoba Departamento de Administracin, where he performed accounting tasks for the Diocese of Crdoba.

Jose's top skills include social media management, Microsoft Office, and public relations. He is fluent in Spanish and has limited working proficiency in Italian and professional working proficiency in English.

Jose holds a degree in Business Administration and Management from Universidad Loyola Andaluca, as well as the completion of two semesters of economics through the Erasmus Program at Sapienza Universit di Roma.
