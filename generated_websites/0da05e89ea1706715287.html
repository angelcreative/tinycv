Miguel

Miguel Cabrera is a Product Designer with experience in various design tools such as Adobe XD, Figma, FigJam, ProtoPie, Notion, Illustrator, Photoshop, After Effects, and Cinema 4D. He is skilled in UX methodologies, including user persona development, wireframing, prototyping, user research, and usability testing.

From June 2019 to June 2020, Miguel worked as a Junior UX/UI Designer at Catapulta Startup SAS, an incubator for app-based ventures in Bogotá, Colombia. During this time, he collaborated closely with the development team and managed a wide range of UI/UX and branding projects for startups.

From June 2020 to December 2020, Miguel continued his role as a Product Designer at Catapulta Startup SAS. He was responsible for selecting and implementing operational processes and tools, as well as providing leadership to the design team.

Miguel has also completed various courses on platforms such as Udemy, including Adobe XD CC, motion graphics design, and professional prototyping. His passion lies in creating user-centered digital products and he prides himself on being a quick learner, always seeking to acquire new skills and tackle interesting challenges.

In his role as a Product Designer at Tiny Ship SA in Panama City, Panama, from February 2021 to October 2021, Miguel worked on the Zappiii app, a multiservice platform available on iOS and Android. He was responsible for enhancing the user experience of the app and collaborated with the graphic design team on audiovisual production, photo editing, content writing, and social media graphic design. He also created animated icons and illustrations using After Effects and LottieFiles to improve the user experience.

From December 2021 to April 2022, Miguel worked as a UI Designer at Pragma SA in Medellín, Colombia. He designed user interfaces for projects assigned by Experian, a Pragma client specializing in credit risk management and fraud. He also worked on improving the candidate experience during the selection process in collaboration with the HR department. Additionally, Miguel conducted talks within the community on new design trends and LowCode/NoCode platforms.

Miguel has worked as a freelance UX/UI Designer, collaborating closely with development teams on a variety of projects for startups. He has extensive experience in creating UI/UX designs from scratch and evaluating product leads to determine which design elements would engage users the most.

Miguel has also contributed his design skills to MATT Manda2 Delivery and Parki, recognized by the Reinventistas program of Bancolombia. He holds a Bachelor's degree in Graphic Design from the Fundación Universitaria del Área Andina in Valledupar, Colombia, and has completed additional courses in UX design on platforms such as Crehana.

At 22 years old, Miguel is a Spanish national currently located in Spain. He is single and can be contacted at uxcabrera@gmail.com or +34 627 30 43 39. His portfolio can be found on Dribbble (dribbble.com/uxcabrera) and Behance (behance.net/uxcabrera).
